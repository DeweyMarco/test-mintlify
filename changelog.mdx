<Update label="2026-04-02" description="New quote and category" tags={["Feature"]}>
  ## New quote and category

  - **New category: `marco original`** — You can now filter quotes by the `marco original` category using the [list quotes](/api-reference/list-quotes) and [random quote](/api-reference/get-random-quote) endpoints.
  - **New quote added** — A new quote by Marco Dewey is available in the API. Fetch it directly with [get quote by ID](/api-reference/get-quote-by-id) or discover it via the random endpoint.
  - The new category also appears in the [list categories](/api-reference/list-categories) response.
</Update>
